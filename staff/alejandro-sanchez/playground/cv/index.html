CV - Hulk

Introduction

Hulk is a powerful and formidable hero with unparalleled strength. Known for his transformations, Hulk is capable of immense feats of strength, making him a key member of the Avengers. Despite his rage, Hulk also seeks to help others, particularly those in need of protection from overwhelming threats.

Experience

Avenger Member (2012-now)

Hulk has been a key member of the Avengers, contributing to the defeat of numerous threats to Earth, including the battle against Loki and the Chitauri, as well as the fight against Ultron. His strength is unmatched, and his rage often fuels his heroic actions.

Gamma Research (2000-2012)

Before becoming Hulk, Bruce Banner, the scientist behind the Hulk transformation, worked on gamma radiation research that ultimately led to the creation of Hulk. His work on this project continues to shape his understanding of his own powers.

Education

PhD in Gamma Radiation (1999)

Dr. Bruce Banner holds a PhD in nuclear physics, specializing in gamma radiation, a field in which he conducted groundbreaking research. This education laid the foundation for his work in becoming the Hulk.

University of Caltech (1994-1999)

Dr. Banner completed his undergraduate studies at the University of Caltech before continuing his research in gamma radiation, ultimately leading to the incident that transformed him into the Hulk.
